Company Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Last Updated: December 15, 2023

1. Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for remote work arrangements at our company.

2. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible to request remote work arrangements. Part-time employees may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

3. Work Hours and Availability
- Remote employees must be available during core business hours (9:00 AM - 3:00 PM EST)
- Employees must respond to communications within 2 hours during business hours
- Overtime must be pre-approved by direct supervisor

4. Equipment and Technology
- Company provides laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse
- Internet connection reimbursement up to $50/month
- Employees are responsible for maintaining secure work environment
- All equipment must be returned upon termination

5. Communication Requirements
- Daily standup meetings via video call at 9:00 AM EST
- Weekly one-on-ones with direct supervisor
- Monthly all-hands meetings (mandatory attendance)
- Use company-approved communication tools only

6. Performance Expectations
- Meet all deadlines and deliverables
- Maintain same productivity levels as in-office work
- Participate actively in team meetings and collaborations
- Complete quarterly performance reviews

7. Security and Confidentiality
- Use VPN at all times when accessing company resources
- No work in public spaces (cafes, libraries, etc.)
- Secure all physical documents and devices
- Report any security incidents immediately

8. Termination of Remote Work
Remote work privileges may be revoked for:
- Poor performance or missed deadlines
- Security violations
- Communication failures
- Business needs requiring in-office presence

Contact: hr@company.com for questions or clarifications.
