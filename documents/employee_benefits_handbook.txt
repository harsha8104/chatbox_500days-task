Employee Benefits Handbook 2024

Welcome to our comprehensive employee benefits package! We're committed to providing competitive benefits that support your health, financial security, and work-life balance.

HEALTH AND WELLNESS BENEFITS

Medical Insurance
We offer three medical plan options through Blue Cross Blue Shield:
- Basic Plan: $0 monthly premium for employee, $500 deductible
- Standard Plan: $50/month premium, $250 deductible, broader network
- Premium Plan: $150/month premium, $100 deductible, premium network
Coverage begins on your first day of employment. Dependent coverage available.

Dental and Vision
- Dental: Two free cleanings per year, 80% coverage for basic procedures
- Vision: One free eye exam annually, $200 frame allowance
Both are fully company-paid for employees.

Mental Health Support
- 12 free therapy sessions per year through BetterHelp
- 24/7 mental health hotline
- Stress management workshops monthly

FINANCIAL BENEFITS

401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company matches 100% up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Roth and traditional options available
- Financial planning consultations included

Stock Purchase Plan
- Purchase company stock at 15% discount
- Payroll deduction available
- No holding period required

Life Insurance
- Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid)
- Supplemental life insurance available up to $500,000
- Spouse and child coverage options

TIME OFF BENEFITS

Vacation Policy
- 0-2 years: 15 days annually
- 3-5 years: 20 days annually
- 6+ years: 25 days annually
Unlimited rollover, use-it-or-lose-it policy waived

Sick Leave
- 10 paid sick days annually
- Can be used for dependent care
- No doctor's note required for single days

Holidays
- 12 paid holidays annually including floating holidays
- Early dismissal before major holidays

Parental Leave
- 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers
- 8 weeks fully paid for secondary caregivers
- Adoption and foster care included
- Gradual return-to-work program

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Tuition Reimbursement
- Up to $5,250 annually for job-related education
- 100% coverage for approved certifications
- Professional conference attendance covered

Internal Training
- LinkedIn Learning license provided
- Monthly lunch-and-learn sessions
- Mentorship program available

WORK-LIFE BENEFITS

Flexible Work Arrangements
- Remote work options (see Remote Work Policy)
- Flexible hours between 7 AM - 7 PM
- Compressed workweeks available

Employee Assistance Program
- Free legal consultations
- Financial planning services
- Childcare and eldercare referrals

Additional Perks
- Free parking or transit pass ($100/month)
- Gym membership reimbursement ($50/month)
- Monthly team building events
- Free snacks and beverages in office

ENROLLMENT AND SUPPORT

New employees must enroll within 30 days of hire. Changes allowed during annual open enrollment or within 30 days of qualifying life events.
