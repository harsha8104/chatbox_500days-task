IT Security Policy and Guidelines

Last Updated: January 2024
Version: 2.1

1. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
All employees must adhere to the following password standards:
- Minimum 12 characters in length
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Cannot contain dictionary words or personal information
- Must be changed every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 12 passwords
- Multi-factor authentication required for all accounts

2. DEVICE SECURITY
Company-Issued Devices:
- Automatic screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Full disk encryption enabled (BitLocker/FileVault)
- Antivirus software must remain active and updated
- Personal software installation requires IT approval
- Devices must be updated within 30 days of patch release

Personal Devices (BYOD):
- Must install company MDM (Mobile Device Management) software
- Work data must be stored in approved containers only
- Devices must pass security scan before network access
- Remote wipe capability required for email access
- Personal devices are subject to audit

3. NETWORK SECURITY
WiFi Access:
- Company WiFi requires WPA3 encryption
- Guest network available for visitors (isolated from internal resources)
- No personal hotspots allowed in office
- VPN required when accessing company resources remotely

Firewall Rules:
- All inbound traffic blocked by default
- Outbound traffic filtered by category
- Social media sites restricted during business hours
- Streaming services blocked except for business purposes

4. EMAIL SECURITY
Phishing Protection:
- All emails scanned by advanced threat protection
- Suspicious emails quarantined automatically
- Employees must report suspicious emails to security@company.com
- Click-time protection enabled for all links

Data Loss Prevention:
- External email warnings for sensitive data
- Automatic encryption for emails containing PII
- Size limits: 25MB per attachment, 100MB total per email
- Personal email forwarding prohibited

5. DATA CLASSIFICATION
Confidential Data:
- Customer personal information
- Financial records and projections
- Trade secrets and proprietary algorithms
- Employee personal information

Internal Data:
- Internal communications
- Project documentation
- Meeting notes and recordings
- Non-sensitive business data

Public Data:
- Marketing materials
- Public website content
- Press releases
- Job postings

6. INCIDENT RESPONSE
Security Incident Reporting:
- Report immediately to security@company.com
- Call emergency line for active threats: 1-800-SECURITY
- Document all relevant details
- Preserve evidence (don't turn off affected systems)

Response Timeline:
- Critical incidents: 15 minutes
- High priority: 1 hour
- Medium priority: 4 hours
- Low priority: 24 hours

7. REMOTE WORK SECURITY
Home Office Requirements:
- Dedicated work space with door that locks
- No family members allowed to use work devices
- Secure storage for physical documents
- Shredder required for confidential documents

Video Conferencing:
- Use company-approved platforms only
- Virtual backgrounds required for home offices
- No recording without explicit permission
- Lock meetings once all participants have joined

8. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
All employees must complete:
- Annual security awareness training
- Quarterly phishing simulations
- Data handling certification
- Incident response training

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

For questions or clarifications, contact IT Security at security@company.com
